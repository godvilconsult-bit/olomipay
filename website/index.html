How it works

Gas at your door in 3 simple steps

No more empty cylinders or queueing at the depot. JIKO CONNECT puts every nearby vendor in your pocket.

01

Find a vendor

We show gas shops near you that actually have your cylinder size in stock — with live prices and ratings.

02

Order & pay

Pick your gas, pay securely with M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money — or cash on delivery. EWURA-fair prices, always.

03

Track to your door

A nearby rider picks it up and delivers. Watch them on a live map and confirm with a one-time delivery code.

Everything in one app

Built for the whole gas journey

From the first tap to the cylinder at your door — and the books behind it — JIKO CONNECT handles it all.

Live GPS tracking

Watch your rider move on a real-time map from pickup to your door, with arrival alerts at every step.

Mobile-money payments

Pay with M-Pesa, Tigo Pesa, Airtel Money or HaloPesa — or cash on delivery. Secure and instant.

EWURA-fair pricing

Prices are capped to EWURA guidance so households always pay a fair, transparent rate for LPG.

Wallets & instant payouts

Every shop, rider and distributor gets a wallet with a full ledger, cash-float tracking and easy cash-out.

Auto-refill subscriptions

Never run out mid-meal. Set a refill schedule and JIKO orders for you automatically before the gas runs low.

Rewards & referrals

Earn loyalty points on every order and bonus credit when friends you invite place their first order.

In-app chat & support

Message your rider or shop directly per order, raise disputes, and get help fast — no phone numbers shared.

Cylinder deposits & returns

Register your cylinders, track deposits, and request a return with the deposit refunded straight to your wallet.

Push & SMS notifications

Stay updated at every stage with web-push alerts, and an SMS fallback so you never miss an order — even offline.
